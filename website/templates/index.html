Gas suffocation

Lack of ventilation is a major danger The authorities warned that the availability of conditions in the heating equipment does not prevent it from remaining a sure source of carbon monoxide emissions. This increases the risk of gas suffocation, which requires adherence to the necessary preventive measures to avoid such accidents

Earthquakes

Earthquakes are a natural phenomenon and occur as a result of an imbalance in the layers of the earth, and their frequency has increased as a result of the increased movement of the crust in previous years. Our product warns of the disaster before it occurs to reduce the largest number of human losses

House fires

The increasing emissions of greenhouse gases as a result of human activity act as a blanket wrapped around the earth, trapping the sun's heat and raising temperatures, resulting in a rise in the rate of house and forest fires.
Our product detects any ignition of fire
